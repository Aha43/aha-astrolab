LS60MT Telescope Configurations

The LS60MT primary use is to observe / imagine the sun in the H-Alpha spectrum of light but it can also be used to observe / imagine the sun in white
light with the H-Alpha filters removed and the Lunt Herschel Diagonal installed or as a regular night telescope using the William Optics Diagonal.

This topic is about showing the different configurations, <i>it is particular important for safety (avoiding damaging eyes or cameras) to be able to 
know when it is safe to use the LS60MT to observe / imagine the sun</i>.
