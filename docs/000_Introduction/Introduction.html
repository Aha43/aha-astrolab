🏠 Home

The aha-astrolab is a personal manual for the astronomical equipment currently in use. The purpose of this site is to serve as a reference for the setup, operation, and capabilities of the gear as it stands today.

The primary focus is solar observation and imaging, with some secondary use for nighttime visual astronomy. Equipment covered includes the Lunt LS60MT solar telescope, SolarQuest and AZ-GTi mounts, ASI174MM camera, and supporting accessories such as the Pentax zoom eyepiece and the Lunt Herschel prism.

The site is structured for practical use, with gear-specific sections, setup guides, and session workflows. It reflects the current operational state of aha-astrolab and is intended both for personal use and as a reference for others who may operate the equipment.
